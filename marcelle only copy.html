What is Duster?

Duster is an American band consisting of multi-instrumentalists Clay Parton, Canaan Dove Amber, and Jason Albertini from California formed in the late 90s.

They are known for their unique, dreamy, melancholic sound.

Their top 3 hit songs are Inside Out, Me and the Birds & Stars will Fall.
